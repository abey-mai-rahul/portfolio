Skills & Interests

✍️

Scriptwriting

I write my scripts in Scenarist, focusing on story structure and clarity. From there, I build mood boards in Milanote to explore tone and atmosphere, then organize everything into clear presentations that help the crew fully understand the vision.

🎥

Cinematography

I plan shots with hand-drawn storyboards. I'm experienced with the Sony FX30, working mainly with Zeiss and Sigma lenses. Lighting Scenes is one of my favorite parts of Cinematography, and I mainly do that using Godox ML100 and LCR500.

🎵

Sound

I record sound using DJI lapels, Rode Shotguns and Zoom recorders. For mixing and music production, I work with FL Studio.

✂️

Editing & Color Grading

I work with Adobe Premiere Pro for editing, Adobe After Effects for VFX, and DaVinci Resolve for color grading, focusing on story telling, pacing, and a cinematic finish.

🎬

Direction

Beyond all the technical aspects, I love working closely with actors. I invest a lot of time in casting and rehearsals, making sure actors understand their characters deeply, sometimes even more than I do. I focus heavily on pre-production so that, once on set, I have the freedom and confidence to experiment and explore performances.
